Page Design

  1. Homepage
    • Purpose: Brief introduction of the site's content and purpose. Mostly empty except with a picture to draw people in.
    • Audience: Anyone in the artistic field needing contacts or people interested in buying artwork.
    • Content: Very general, meant to be aesthetic and show off the overall feel of the client's art Very few actual words on this page.
    • User input: None
    • Validation: None
    • Buttons, etc.: Menu or Links to the other pages of the website
    • Page actions: Links will take the user to each subpage, maybe a banner of pictures.
  2. About Me
    • Purpose: Background story on my client, personal information about them, any information they wish to share.
    • Audience: Anyone interested in learning about the artist behind the art
    • Content: Pictures of the artist and information blobs, likely to be paragraphs and bullet points with associated pictures.
    • User input: None
    • Validation: None
    • Buttons, etc.: Potential buttons to scroll through information if it get's that long, otherwise none
    • Page actions: Button will change pages of infomation. Links to other subpages.
  3. E-Portfolio Page
    • Purpose: A showcasing of their artworks.
    • Audience: Anyone interested in viewing the client's artwork.
    • Content: A gallery of their existing artworks.
    • User input: None
    • Validation: None
    • Buttons, etc.: Buttons to change content, links
    • Page actions: Button will change content and text to the specific class. Links to main page and next catagory.
  4. Contact Information
    • Purpose: Displays email address and social media platforms to contact the client at
    • Audience: Those interested talking to the client at all.
    • Content: Information fields for the client's information and clickable hyperlinks to send to social medias.
    • User input: None
    • Validation: None
    • Buttons, etc.: Buttons to change pages and go to various social media platforms.
    • Page actions: Button will change page and other buttons will send to other websites for social media.
  5. Commissions
    • Purpose: Show the current status of commissions, whether the client is taking commissions, and prices of commissions with examples.
    • Audience: Those interested buying the client's artwork.
    • Content: Information on whether or not the client is taking commissions, working on commissions, and current prices of commissions.
    • User input: Potentially a place to fill out user information with what they would like from a commissions
    • Validation: None
    • Buttons, etc.: Buttons to change pages, a field to fill out with commission information, and switch to other pages in the website.
    • Page actions: Button will allow switching from pages and a submit button to submit commission information.
  6. Donations
    • Purpose: Those that wish to support the client with donations or other means
    • Audience: Those interested supporting the client through other means than buying art.
    • Content: A link to a patreon page, Ko-fi page, and a venmo and cashapp handle.
    • User input: None
    • Validation: None
    • Buttons, etc.: Buttons to go to other pages, links to other websites with the associated information
    • Page actions: Button will switch what page the user is on, links will lead to other websites.
  7. Dynamic Functionality: Some of the interactive features this website will contain are: Buttons that link to other pages, buttons that lead to external platforms of the client, and some clickable scroll / media gallery features to display pictures in the portfolio. There will be a forms page on the contact me and commissions pages.
